TPL

Description

Embrace your ideas at Tensor Premiere League (Tpl), an innovative competition hosted by St.Joseph's TensorCamp. Contestants are given a set of use cases by TensorFlow collaborators, tasking them with creating inventive ideas to address these challenges. Participants are chosen by the collaborators according to the creativity of their concepts.

Following the selection phase, the selected students are grouped into teams according to their specific problem statements. The teams chosen are dedicated to assisting the collaborator's project. Mentors are assigned to students to offer support and direction during the duration of the project. These externships enable the students to acquire practical experience in a real-world setting.
